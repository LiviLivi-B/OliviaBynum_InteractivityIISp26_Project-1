I highly value the art direction of video games, video games are capable of completely immersing you in it's world. Unfortunately, this part of development is often overlooked. I believe that art direction is important for making a game stand out.


ENA Dream BBQ

  • Genre: Adventure
  • Release Date: March 27, 2025
  • Developers: Joel G and ENA Team

This is a wonderfully bizarre adventure game where you explore the strange world of ENA. I've always loved surrealism so this game is up my alley. It’s almost incomprehensible but that’s exactly what makes it fun to play through. (Also it is free too so check it out!)

The World Ends With You

  • Genre: Action Rpg
  • Release Date: July 27, 2007
  • Developers: Square Enix

This classic DS game was heavily inspired by graffiti art and street fashion. It has a timeless look and it really embodies this grungy street aesthetic. The bold sharp lineart is one of the highlights of this game’s art design.
