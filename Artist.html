Here are the artist who inspired me thoughout the years!

Dominique Ramsey

Dominique Ramsey is a freelance illustrator based in North Carolina. She's great at capturing bold colors and eyecatching textures. A lot of her works also include stylized animals with designs that stand out.


Smiling Faces 2019
Sojourners Magazine / Animals
Love is Just a Game 2023

Tracy Butler

Tracy J. Butler is the creator, artist, and writer of the comic Lackadaisy. Their art features lively characters with amazing expressions. The way the color and shade is reminiscent of early twentieth century artists, (think J.C Leynndecker). The backgrounds in their work are so vast and detailed it is breathtaking.
